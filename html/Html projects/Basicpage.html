About Me

Our bike garage provides a comprehensive range of services to keep your bike in top condition. We specialize in repairs, from fixing flats and adjusting brakes to more complex issues like wheel truing and frame repairs. In addition to repairs, we offer routine maintenance, including tune-ups, chain cleaning, and brake or gear adjustments to ensure your bike runs smoothly. We also carry a selection of high-quality bike parts and accessories, such as tires, tubes, helmets, and locks. Whether you're looking to customize your bike with upgrades or need expert advice, our team is here to help. Visit us for personalized service and expert guidance to keep your bike performing at its best!
